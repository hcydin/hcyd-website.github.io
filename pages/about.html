Executive Team

Xavier Lewis | President

As President of the Hamilton County Young Democrats I want to build a group of other like-minded young people and give them the tools and access to candidates, campaigns, and having their concerns heard in government. As a demographic we have been chronically under activated and under represented in government. It's time for that to change. When I'm not working on politics I love to make things with me hands, whether that’s a costume for a renaissance faire, a new tool with my 3D printer, or making a birthday gift using leather.

Jo May | Vice President

Natalie Smith | Secretary

Alex Waclawik | Treasurer

As the Treasurer, my primary goal is to keep the organization in good financial health, but I also take care of other things such as event planning, managing the technology the group uses, and recruitment. In my spare time, I enjoy going to see local bands, working on my car, going to the shooting range, or one of my many personal projects.

Kim Qian | Comms Chair

Sarah Fix | Events Chair

As the event committee chair, I want to help our members get involved in their communities and develop relationships with each other. I learn things about a community or environment best when I interact with it - which is why I love hiking, traveling, going out to eat, and participating in protests and clean up projects. I like to explore parks like Potters Bridge and Brown County State Park with my two dogs, Evie and Rosie, and I love getting ice cream at Alexander’s on the Square.
