Who Are We?

The Hamilton County Young Democrats of Indiana (HCYD) are a civic youth organization dedicated to widening the interest, knowledge and participation of young people in government and the political process. We aim to train and develop young people for positions of leadership and to serve those in need in our community. We strive to increase awareness and activism on issues of importance to young people and increase the role of under-represented people in the Democratic Party, government and society. HCYD represents the cities of Carmel, Fishers, Noblesville and Westfield alongside the towns of Cicero, Arcadia, Atlanta and Sheridan. Together we are leading the way forward in Indiana for progressive politics and are the backbone of local grassroots political campaigns for Democratic candidates in Hamilton County. Together the Hamilton County Young Democrats show that #YoungGetsItDone!

Monthly Meetings & Events

HCYD meets every month at rotating locations in all four major cities. While these meetings are primarily for socializing, they can also include updates from the executive team, presentations on policy or political issues, guest speakers, or group discussions.

In addition to monthly meetings, HCYD hosts a number of events each month. These range from fun events to community service. Along with hosting, HCYD will also attend other political and non-political events as a group. Lastly, you don't need to be an HCYD member to attend these event. Everyone is welcome.
